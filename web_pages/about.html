SEFWhite (Sho_GD) is an independant creator.

Sho_GD operates as a game designer, programmer and artist.

Originally from the UK and has been living in Japan for the last 8 years.

Sho_GD (SEFWHITE, sho-gd) aims to provide Video Games as a downloadable service,
as well as contents creation and promotional marketing of said Video Games,
through the use of social media platforms such as Youtube, TikTok, Twitter.

Refund and dispute policy:

In compliance with the distribution platform used to provide Video Games,
refunds and disputes must be handled through said platforms.
Example: A game release on the Google Play Store under Sho_GD,
complies with the Play Store's refund and dispute policy, however Sho_GD will not be able to provide the refund themselves.
Please refer to that Store front's customer support for any enquiries.
